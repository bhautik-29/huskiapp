Greso's Spirit

Deeply Parisian and contemporary, Suncoo was born in 2010 from the
meeting of two aesthetes : Stella and Thomas. It inspires a strong wind
of freedom to a femininine wardrobe ! Each season, Stella, designer and
artistic director imagines delicate and characterized silhouettes which
are daring and freedom prints. Bohemian knitwear, flimsy fabrics, Mix &
Match, colours schemes, vibrant prints, femininine details... Suncoo's
collections accompany women in their daily life to enhance them
better. Free, elegant, wanderlust in her life as through her style,
Suncoo women distils pieces which look like herself and which give in
her daily all energy she needs. She is this perfect ambassador of
effortless mythic chic.
